Research: A Plan to Discover Fundamental Principles of Intelligence

"The intelligence of a system is a measure of its skill-acquisition efficiency over a scope of tasks, with respect to priors, experience, and generalization difficulty". - Francois Chollet. For further reference, see On the Measure of Intelligence.
For now, taking the definition of intelligence as provided, I am writing a research paper proposing a new paradigm for AI (un-named for now) such as need for embodiement, general task (prediction), misalignment between input & output dimensions, need for self generated open ended abstractions, dynamcis v/s statistics, and much more. Furthermore working on a research paper on "How to write research paper in this un-named new paradigm of AI?".

I am expanding on the research paper Predictive Vision Model(PVM) by Filip Piekniewski et al. and measure the intelligence of it compared to CNN, Transformer etc. In PVM, Filip proposes a new paradigm of AI based on dynamics not statistics.

To know more, refer to Filip's website and read all his blogs. Concisely, the wisdom and exciting set of ideas in those blogs is the best in the field of AI I have found till now.

I am taking a deep dive in the field of contemporary neuroscience which would take 4-5 years and use it for inspiration to create new AI systems. Also, how to encode Human Priors as specified in "On the Measure of Intelligence" is an unsolved problem for now.

Intelligence's first necessity is embodiement. "Embodiement is constant access to the real world through a high bandwidth sensory input." - Kshitij Parikh.

To solve intelligence, I am going to start with the frontier of perception inspired from the fundamental problem in AI i.e. Moravec's Paradox. Dexterity and Mobility will come in soon due to affordances being necessary for perception. Language will come very later. Language is a tool for communication and thought but not necessary for intelligence.

If you goal is to solve Intelligence, let's connect.
